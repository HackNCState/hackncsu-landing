Hack_NCState is North Carolina State University's premier hackathon.

A 24-hour race to crack the case.

This ain't no ordinary gig. It's a 24-hour investigation where operatives collaborate to solve problems using technology. Whether you're a seasoned PI or a rookie gumshoe, you’ll form squads, build your case from scratch - like websites, apps, or hardware, and bring your solution to light. Grab your crew and start digging for clues!

The Agency covers the tab. Hack_NCState is 100% free for all operatives to attend! Enjoy complimentary rations, a standard-issue t-shirt, stickers, and other activities while you're on the case.

When's the operation?

The operation runs through the weekend of February 14th and 15th. You start cracking the case at 11 AM on Saturday and have until 11 AM on Sunday to submit your findings.

location_on

Where's the rendezvous?

The operation is held in Talley Student Union in the heart of North Carolina State University’s Main Campus.

person

Who's on the case?

This case is open to all university students. You don't need to be an NC State student to join the investigation!

Debriefing

What's the MO?

Squads of 2-4 operatives will work together to build a project and present their findings to the chief. This project can be or do anything; there are no constraints on what you're allowed to develop, as long as the project fits your squad's chosen dossier. The chief will then select the best overall. Every part of the case must be cracked at the event - no outside intel or help is permitted.

What if I'm a lone wolf?

No problem. We'll have a briefing early in the day where you can connect with other operatives who are looking to fill out or form a squad. This is a perfect chance to expand your network or link up with new contacts.

What's a dossier?

A dossier helps focus your investigation on one specific subject area. You can think of it like a case category. Some examples of dossiers include fintech, cybersecurity, or infrastructure.

What's the damage?

Zero. The Agency covers all expenses. What’s more, as an operative you’ll receive a standard-issue t-shirt, stickers, and rations, alongside the opportunity to participate in special workshops and activities. However, this does mean we are unable to provide any travel compensation.

What's the timeline?

The operation schedule and other key intel will be sent out to registered operatives as we get closer to the event. If you’re registered, you can view all your case files on today.hackncstate.org as they’re released.
